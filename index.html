About us

Message from the Principal

Mrs. Priyanka Kumari

SBCS

Looking forward for your support… A grand greeting to all our stakeholders who believe in the power of true knowledge. Sri Bhagwan Chinta Mani School aspires to “Empower all Students to Succeed in their World.” We offer a wide variety of challenging curricular opportunities, athletic programs, performing arts, musical programs and various activities.

It is our belief that school should be “The Experience of a Lifetime.” High school experiences set the stage for success later in life and create lifelong positive memories for our students.

Our dream is to create wise and knowledgeable individuals with strong, open and judicious minds. We aim to nurture the most valued treasure of a nation by imparting holistic education and values so that students become invaluable assets to society.

Through our school and our children, I foresee a world with discerning individuals who are not only knowledgeable but also worldly wise, capable of creating a new and better world.

Featured by us

Library

Our school library provides a rich collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support learning and research. It encourages students to develop reading habits and expand their knowledge beyond the classroom.

Laboratory

The school laboratories are well equipped for practical learning in science subjects. Students gain hands-on experience through experiments that help them understand scientific concepts clearly.

School Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education combining academic excellence, creativity, and character development. It helps students grow intellectually, socially, and morally.

Computer Education

Computer education is an integral part of our learning system. Students are trained in digital skills, programming basics, and technology awareness to prepare them for the modern world.
